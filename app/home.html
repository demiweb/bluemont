Bluemont is a Belgian investment holding company

From the outset, the company’s founders shared a belief that the synergy of shipping and modern online technology would level the playing field, allowing thousands of businesses and professionals to leverage innovation and technology to grow and compete more effectively in the national and global economy. Bluemont's core businesses include investing in international cargo shipping services, cloud technology, innovation initiatives and others.

Bluemont is headquartered in Antwerp, Belgium and its diversified business operations are represented all over the world and its qualified employees come from a variety of nationalities. The group reflects dynamism in its multiple business sectors while maintaining the identity and values of a strong enterprise. Bluemont is always keen to increase its global market presence and is open to new acquisitions and strong partnerships.

Varamar

Varamar is a shipping company established back in 2009 by Alexander Varvarenko. 

Varamar is well known as a carrier dealing with breakbulk, heavy & oversized, and dry-bulk cargo. 
For over a decade, we have developed transport solutions for dismantled plants, factories, wind turbines, bridges, vehicles and even stadiums. We have developed our own methods based on in-house competences and provide a strong set-up for worldwide shipments. 
We are also involved in tramp shipments of dry-bulk cargo and containers.
